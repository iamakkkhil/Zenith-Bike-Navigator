Built For The Open Road

Every feature in Zenith is designed to enhance your motorcycle ride experience without getting in your way.

Smart Handlebar Joystick Control

Keep your hands on the bars. Control your phone's music (play, pause, next/prev track) directly using the bike's physical handlebar joystick. Dial calls, accept or reject alerts, and sync updates seamlessly.

Tripper Dash Notification Sync

Stay informed without distractions. Get real-time WhatsApp notifications, SMS updates, and incoming caller IDs displayed directly on your bike's Tripper console display while riding.

The Glovebox (Garage Scanner)

Say goodbye to carrying wet paper documents. Safely store and manage digital copies of registration, insurance policies, and service invoices inside the app, immediately ready to show.

Battery-Optimized Ride Logging

Log long day rides without a powerbank. High-precision GPS logging is optimized using SQLite indexing and background thread updates, keeping battery draw to an absolute minimum.

100% Offline Vector Maps

Navigate securely even when mobile signal drops. Ambient tile caching stores map components locally, enabling route lines, maps, and waypoints to render instantly offline.

Interactive Strava-Style Share Cards

Create stunning visual summaries of your rides. Overlay your stats and route on top of custom background photos. Fully customizable: drag and pinch-zoom route, stats, or watermark to fit.
