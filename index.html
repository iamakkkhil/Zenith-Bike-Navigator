Step 1 of 6

Step 2 of 6

Confirm Browser Alert

If prompted by your browser with a security warning, tap OK or Download anyway to proceed.

Step 3 of 6

Open File Manager

Once the download is complete, open your device's File Manager or Downloads folder and tap the `zenith-latest.apk` file.

Step 4 of 6

Enable Sideload Permissions

If this is your first time sideloading, your device will prompt you. Tap Settings and switch on Allow from this source for your browser or file manager.

Step 5 of 6

If Google Play Protect warns you or blocks installation (common for community builds with self-signed keys), you may need to temporarily pause it. Click here to learn how to pause Play Protect.
